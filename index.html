Meme STORY

ORIGIN

The colloquial Spanish phrase "me gusta" was first adopted into a Rage Comic drawing by illustrator May Oswald, who uploaded her artwork to 4chan and Reddit on March 18th, 2010. The drawing was also uploaded to her personal Tumblr blog and DeviantART account.

SPREADING

The first meme featuring Me Gusta after Oswald's initial post to 4chan used the character in a four-panel comic with a cat reacting to being pet, which Oswald revealed in our interview was actually a meme she made herself.

Legend

In December 2010, the single topic blog Me Gusta Face was launched to curate the comics. As of May 2012, the series ranked in the "Fuck Yeah" tier on Memegenerator and thousands of "Me Gusta" rage comics can be found on Tumblr, Memebase and on Reddit's popular F7U12 subreddit.
